About Us

  • Our warm and welcoming atmosphere provides a friendly, safe and secure environment for your child to play, learn and grow


  • Suitable for ages 0-5 years


  • Art & craft, story time, singing & play dough


  • Pavilion building with fans, air con & heating


  • Large enclosed outdoor area with cubby house, playground, sandpit and bike track


  • Incursions/excursions each term such as animal farms, play centres, performers & Christmas party with Santa


  • Themed craft activities for Easter, Mother's Day, Father's Day & Christmas

Our Playgroup is run by a committee of volunteers of it's members whose goal is to make Playgroup an enjoyable experience for all members. Along with the committee positions, each session has a Session Leader whose role is to run their session. We always welcome any feedback or suggestions on ways to improve our Playgroup communicated through the Committee.

We are in the gated Pavilion behind the basketball courts, please enter from the gate closest to the School playground.

Grandparents, other family members or carers are welcome to bring children to Playgroup, it does not have to be mum or dad.

Our Sessions

Session Times

Monday to Friday: 9.15 - 11:15am subject to availability.

Parents/Guardian must attend with the Child.

What do I Bring?

Please bring a water bottle, hat, a piece of Fruit to Share (optional) or your own morning tea for your children. As we have some children with severe allergies, please avoid bringing food containing nuts.

Bring a Toy or Book for the Treasure Bag. Treasure Bag is a bag where we put your item in and pull it out during Song Time for your child to identify as their item and have something special to take home.

Costs

Fees are non refundable

$35 per term for first child (based on school terms), $10 per child thereafter per term based on attending same week day each week.

$35 per family Playgroup WA annual membership fee ($15 concession card).
